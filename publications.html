Research - Current Focus

PhD · Cybersecurity & Remote Engineering

My research explores how to secure remote engineering systems built on IoT and IIoT technologies, with emphasis on anomaly detection and policy-enforced, identity-aware architectures.

  • IoT/IIoT testbeds for cybersecurity experimentation – design and evaluation of modular testbeds with MQTT, Node-RED, InfluxDB, and Grafana.
  • Real-time MQTT-based intrusion detection – feature selection, model training, and deployment of ML-based IDS on edge devices using datasets such as MQTTset.
  • Zero Trust Ntework Access – identity-aware access control, mTLS, and policy enforcement for remote engineering applications.
  • Educational remote labs – integrating secure remote access and monitoring into engineering education environments.
